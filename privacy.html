Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 2025

This privacy policy explains how the Call YANIV! mobile app handles user information.

Information We Collect

Call YANIV! does not collect, store, or share any personal data. All game data, including player names and scores, is stored locally on your device and permanently deleted when the app is uninstalled.

Analytics and Tracking

The app does not use analytics, advertising, or third-party tracking tools.

Data Security

Because no personal data is collected or transmitted, there are no external servers or databases involved. All processing happens directly on your device.

Changes to This Policy

If we make updates to this privacy policy, the new version will be published on this page with a revised “Last updated” date.
