Call YANIV! — Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Call YANIV! handles information. We designed the app to be private by default.

Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or share any personal data. All game information (e.g., player names, scores, round history) is stored locally on your device and is permanently removed when you uninstall the app.

Analytics, Ads & Tracking

The app does not use analytics, advertising, third-party SDKs, or tracking technologies.

Data Processing & Security

  • All processing happens on your device; no external servers or databases are used.
  • No network transmission of your game data occurs.

Children’s Privacy

The app does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this Policy, we will publish the new version on this page and revise the “Last updated” date above.

Contact

Questions about privacy? Contact us at callyaniv@gmail.com.
